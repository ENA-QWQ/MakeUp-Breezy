# MakeUp Breezy
This is a modified version of [MakeUp-UltraFast](https://github.com/javiergcim/MakeUpUltraFast) by javiergcim. Original version 9.5a. Modifications by ENA_QWQ.

## Version: 9.5a-reedited

### Works with OptiFine and Iris 1.5.1+.

## Changes from original

MakeUp-Breezy gives Minecraft a cel‑shaded, anime‑like look.
- Replaced smooth lighting with luminance‑based binary thresholding. Dark areas shift to purple, bright areas get boosted blue tone.
- Candle colors are quantized to 16 luma levels
- Specular highlights from glossy materials are removed.
- Black outlines based on depth differences.
- Clouds turn magenta during dawn and dusk.
- Increased saturation and contrast of the sky, especially at sunrise and sunset.
